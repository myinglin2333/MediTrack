About MediTrack

MediTrack is a simple medication tracking app for families and patients.

Here, you can manage your medications, track daily intake, and keep refill records all in one place.

How to Use

  1. Add your medications - Click Manage Medications to register a medication with its dosage and schedule.
  2. Mark medications as taken - On the Manage Medications page, click Mark Taken after you take a dose.
  3. Edit or delete medications - Use the Edit and Delete buttons in the medications table to keep your list current.
  4. Log refills - Go to Refill Records to add a new refill with the medication name, date, quantity, and pharmacy.
  5. Edit or delete refills - Use the Edit and Delete buttons in the refill history table to update or remove records.
  6. Review refill history - Browse all past refills in the history table to keep track of your prescriptions.
